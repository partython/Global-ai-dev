# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Priya AI Technologies ("Company," "we," "us," "our") operates Priya Global, an AI-powered omnichannel customer communication platform ("Service," "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service. By accessing and using Priya Global, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

**Business Data**
- Customer lists and contact information

**Communication Data**
- Messages, emails, and conversations sent through the Platform

**Content You Upload**
- Documents, templates, and knowledge bases
- Custom configurations and business rules
- Training data for AI models (opt-in)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Analytics**
- Pages visited and features used
- Time spent on the Platform

- Unique identifiers and session cookies

**Log Data**
- HTTP requests and response codes
- Timestamps of activities
- Referrer information

**Communications Metadata**
- Message timestamps and recipient information
- Channel information (email, SMS, WhatsApp, etc.)
- Delivery and read receipts
- Response times and engagement metrics

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- Data from payment processors (Stripe)
- Information from cloud infrastructure providers (AWS)
- Integration data from connected services
- Publicly available business information

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Account Data
- Retained while your account is active

- Backed up for disaster recovery purposes

### 5.2 Post-Termination Deletion
- 90 days after account closure or deletion request

- Anonymized, aggregated data retained indefinitely

- Compliance data retained per legal requirements
